Experimental details

  • For the Training and Testing of the Model IndicTTS Dataset was used.
  • One pair of Male and Female speakers was chosen for both Hindi and Marathi Languages. For each speaker approximately 20 minutes of data was used for Training purpose
  • No Neural vocoders were used. The feature Extraction and speech re-synthesis was done using the WORLD vocoder.
